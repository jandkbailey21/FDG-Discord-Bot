Fantasy Disc Golf (FDG) SMS Alerts – Privacy Policy

Fantasy Disc Golf (FDG) provides optional SMS alerts to members of a private fantasy sports league.

Information We Collect

  • Mobile phone number
  • Team name within the league
  • Alert subscription preferences

How We Use This Information

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver transactional SMS alerts related to league activity, including roster updates, waiver awards, event withdrawal notifications, and lineup reminders. We do not use phone numbers for marketing.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile numbers with third parties. Messaging delivery is handled through Twilio solely to deliver alerts.

Opt-Out

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message or disabling SMS alerts within the league dashboard.
